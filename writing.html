2019-2022 novel · unfinished

Ravens and Demigods

In my Freshman year of highschool I started working on a novel, I worked on it sophmore and junior year, it grew to some 160 pages, but it fell aside when higher priorities came with tighter deadlines. I wrote most of whats published here when I was 14-15, so its a bit dramatic. It is an examination of utilitarianism and self righteous justification of terrible things done for a greater good. It's a scifi story, about a scientist's vision of what humanity could be, how it should be, and how he loses his humanity in pursuit of it.
